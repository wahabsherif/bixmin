Do you deliver building materials nationwide in Nigeria?
Yes,Bixmind Global Resources Limited offers fast and reliable nationwide delivery across Nigeria, including Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt and other major cities.
What types of building materials do you supply?
We supply a wide range of building materials including cement, sharp sand, granite, iron rods, blocks and other essential construction materials
How much do building materials cost in Nigeria?
The cost of building materials in Nigeria varies depending on the product and quantity. ContactBixmind Global Resources Limited for current prices and bulk order discounts
Can I order building materials in bulk?
Yes, we specialize in bulk supply for contractors, developers and individuals. We offer competitive pricing for large orders.
How do I get a quote for building materials?
You can request a quote by calling us directly or sending a message via WhatsApp. Our team will respond quickly with pricing and delivery details.
Do you supply building materials in Lagos?
Yes, we are based in Lekki, Lagos and supply building materials across Lagos and nationwide.
Are your products sourced locally or imported?
As a leading builders merchant in Lagos, we offer a wide range of high-quality building materials sourced both locally and internationally. We partner with reputable manufacturers in Nigeria to provide locally made cement, sand, and iron rods, and we also import premium materials and specialised construction tools. This ensures our customers have access to durable and cost-effective options for their projects.
